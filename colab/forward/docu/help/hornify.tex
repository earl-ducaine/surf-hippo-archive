\stmb{hornify}:

Format: \stmt{hornify}

Options: none.

Effect:	All rules of \stmt{*rules-database*}, \stmt{*up-rulebase*} and
	\stmt{*rl-rulebase*} will be hornified.
	Hornified means that a rule with more than one conclusion is 
	splitted into several rules, each with one conclusion.
	
	
see also: \stmb{fw-compile}, \stmt{hornify-up}
