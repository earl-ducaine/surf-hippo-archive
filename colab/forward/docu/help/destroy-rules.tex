\stmb{destroy-rules:}

Format: \stmt{destroy-rules}

Options: none

Effect:  Destroy all databases with rules in \FORWARD{}. Their names are 
         \stmt{*rule-database*}, \stmt{*up-rulebase*}, \stmt{*hn-rulebase*}, \stmt{*forward-rules*} 
	 and \stmt{*magic-rules*}.

see also: \consult, \destroy, \replace
