\stmb{semi-naive}:

Format: \stmt{semi-naive}

Options: none

Effect: Semi-naive bottom-up evaluation is performed on the databases 
	\stmt{*rule-database*} and \stmt{*factbase*}.  The derived facts are added to the 
	database \stmt{*derived-factbase*}.

see also: \stmb{magic-eval}, \stmb{magic-semi}
